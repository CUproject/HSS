## European Type Jaw Crusher
The crushing method of European type jaw crusher is dynamic squeeze. The working principle is: the electromotor drives the belt and pulley to make the movable jaw move back and forth, up and down through the eccentric shaft. The raw material is squashed or split when movable jaw plate moves towards fixed jaw together with movable jaw.
